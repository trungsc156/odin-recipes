Spaghetti

How to make delicious spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground turkey
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Cook ground turkey in pan
  2. Chop onions and cook in pan
  3. Mix turkey and onions
  4. Pour in tomato sauce
  5. Cook for 30 min
  6. Boil spaghetti noodle for 11 min
